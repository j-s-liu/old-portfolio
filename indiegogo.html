ABOUT

In the Spring 2016 Semester, through Berkeley Innovation, I worked on a consulting project for Indiegogo, creating a new profile for entrepreneurs on the crowdfunding site. Working directly with Indiegogo, my team and I conducted user research, created various iterations of sketches and wireframes, ran user testing, and presented hi-fidelity mockups to Indiegogo's headquarters in SF.

I worked with Julia Liang, Vanessa Ng, and Ken Chen on this project. We were mentored by Bianca Romulo.
