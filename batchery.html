I worked as a graphic design intern at Batchery from February 2016 to August 2016.

Batchery is a startup co-working space and incubator located in Oakland, CA, currently hosting 34 startups and 50 investor-advisors. The startups work in industries including hardware, productivity software, mobile apps, consumer products. The investor-advisors come from diverse professional backgrounds, serving as Angel Investors and venture capitalists, or having worked at companies like Google, Oracle, and Accenture.

At Batchery, I worked mainly on marketing materials and the company's website.
